> Pedro Baez's contract story

Pedro Baez's contract   
: signing bonus - 500k
: 2021 - 4.5M
: 2022 - 5.5M + 1M(option IP)
: 2023 - 7.5M(club option) + 2M(buyout); (8M club option and 2.5M buyout if he logs 100 IP between 2021 and 2022)
